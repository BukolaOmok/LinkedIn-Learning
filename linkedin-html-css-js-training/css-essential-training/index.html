Bukola Omokehinde

Peak Performance Educator, Serial Entrepreneur and Investor

Bukola Omokehinde is a Peak Performance Educator and an Organisational Psychology professional, recognised as a Global Talent in the United Kingdom for harnessing technology to empower and educate young people. With a blend of professional expertise and academic insights, she offers evidence-based peak performance guidance to over 1300 newsletter readers.

Bukola started her career at Bank of America, United Kingdom where she worked on the Futures Options and OTC Clearing desk. At Bank of America, she trained for and became a qualified people development professional by the Chartered Institute of People & Development. She then transitioned into the technology industry at Palantir as a Talent Program Manager.

Bukola has executed several Youth Development Projects in Africa including Growdiance, an education platform empowering young ambitious Africans with the tools and resources to harness their potential. Through this project, she set up the Growth Podcast, recognised by Apple Podcast as the number 1 most listened to Self Improvement Podcast in Nigeria. On the podcast, she shares the remarkable journeys of Professionals and Entrepreneurs with African Backgrounds to inspire the next generation of corporate leaders and entrepreneurs. Previous guests include a Director at Bank of America, a Director at Amazon and an Ex Senior Executive at Meta.

Bukola is currently studying an advanced degree program part-time in Organisational Psychology at the University of London; and loves to read, think, and write. You may also find her taking long walks in nature and debating about culture and society.
